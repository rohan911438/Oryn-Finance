## Next Step: Fund Your Testnet Account

Your wallet address needs to be funded on the Stellar testnet before you can see balances.

### How to Get Testnet Funds (Free XLM):

1. **Copy your wallet address** from the connected wallet button in the navbar

2. **Visit Stellar's Friendbot**:
   - Go to: https://laboratory.stellar.org/#account-creator?network=test
   - OR: https://friendbot.stellar.org

3. **Paste your address** and click "Get test network lumens"

4. **Wait a few seconds** for the transaction to complete

5. **Refresh your app** - you should now see your XLM balance!

### Alternative Method:

Visit the Stellar Laboratory:
```
https://laboratory.stellar.org/#account-creator?network=test
```

Paste your Freighter wallet address and click "Get test network lumens"
